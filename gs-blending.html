Often the best way to make a series of models and smoothly vary the modulation parameters.

But there is a way to blend/average/morph two models with user-defined weights.

Make a smooth model and one perturbed by noise. Then blend those to different degrees. This is the only way to do it because the noise is random. (Or you could make a matrix of noise and use that as a bump map.)
