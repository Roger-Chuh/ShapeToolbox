******************************
Getting started with |toolbox|
******************************

This getting started -section demonstrates the functionality of
|toolbox| by short, simple examples.  They give an overview of the
possible shapes and modulations of those shapes.  The will also get
you started with defining material properties and textures, blending
models, batch processing, and so forth.

For examples of more complete projects, see :ref:`tutorials`.
